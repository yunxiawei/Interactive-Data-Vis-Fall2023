Interactive Data Visualization Final Project

I'm visualizing diverse facets of the art world, using datasets encompassing artists' nationalities, genders, and departmental representation. I'm using these graphs to show the colorful mix of nationalities in art, the numbers of male and female artists, and which types of art are most popular. I'm doing this because I want everyone to see how diverse and exciting art can beâ€”like a colorful map showing where artists are from and what they create!

Data Clean: I filtered out rows containing empty gender or nationality information, retaining only the entries with filled values.

1. Nationalities by Gender (Bar Chart): This visualization provides a comprehensive overview of artists' nationalities distributed by gender. Through a bar chart representation, it showcases the count of artists from diverse nationalities categorized by gender. It presents the gender-based distribution of artists across different nationalities, enabling a quick understanding of the gender composition within various cultural contexts.

2. Treemap by Nationalities (Treemap Visualization): The treemap visualization illustrates the distribution of artists' nationalities, offering the flexibility to filter by gender. It presents a hierarchical display where the sizes of the rectangles indicate the count of artists, grouped by nationality. It aims to provide an interactive exploration of artists' nationalities, allowing users to analyze the gender distribution within each nationality category and gain insights into the proportional representation of different nationalities.

3. Artworks by Department (Bar Chart): This bar chart visualization offers a comparative view of artworks across various departments. It highlights the count of artworks within each department, emphasizing the most and least represented departments through color differentiation. This visualization facilitates a quick assessment of the artistic concentration within different departments, drawing attention to the spectrum of representation among diverse art categories, with the Highlight most represented department with green and highlighted least represented department with red.
